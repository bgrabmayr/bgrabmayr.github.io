Balthasar Grabmayr

I am a Junior Professor of Philosophy at Tübingen University. Before that, I was an Azrieli International Postdoctoral Fellow in the Department of Philosophy at the University of Haifa and concurrently held a postdoctoral position at the Blavatnik School of Computer Science at Tel Aviv University. I received my PhD in Philosophy from Humboldt University of Berlin. My research mostly focuses on logic and the philosophies of logic, mathematics, and computation.
